# HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce distribution, employee demographics, and job satisfaction metrics.

The dashboard helps HR teams and management identify key factors influencing employee attrition and supports data-driven workforce planning and retention strategies.

# Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends across departments
* Monitor workforce demographics and employee distribution
* Identify high-risk attrition groups
* Track job satisfaction and employee engagement
* Provide actionable HR insights for decision-making

---

# Insights Generated

* Highest attrition observed in **Sales** and **R&D** departments
* Employees aged **35–44** showed higher attrition rates
* Employees with lower job satisfaction contributed more to attrition
* Single employees showed relatively higher attrition compared to married employees
